Sunny Sweet

Chief Marketing Officer

Sunny Sweet has been a leader in numerous entrepreneurial ventures for the past 40 years, most notably, CEO/Founder of Reunion Celebrations, Inc., reunion planner in the United States, the industries giant. Reunion Business Consultants, Faux MasterPieces, and Sweet Spots Marketing were also and founded and administrated by Ms. Sweet. She has been offering her consultation services to companies and organizations, addressing their business needs for more than 20 years. Her services have encompassed writing and implementing strategic marketing and business plans, creative business branding, enhancing companies' image, and assisting with the multitude of business issues that may arise from start-up, implementation, or planning for an exit strategy.

Before co-founding YourSocialOffers.com, Ms. Sweet's latest venture was an administrative consultant position for a multi-unit and multi-international franchise, responsible for the inception of their business strategy, personnel development, operational and overall supervisory responsibilities.

Ms. Sweet has volunteered and worked vigorously for non-profit organizations, acting as President and Vice-President with Mothers Against Drunk Driving (MADD), granting wishes to children at Make-A-Wish Foundation, and adult volunteer for Moffatt Cancer Center.

Ms. Sweet received the honor of being recognized as one of five finalists of Entrepreneur of the Year sponsored by Inc. Magazine and one of five finalists Small Business of the year, sponsored by the Greater Tampa Chamber of Commerce.
